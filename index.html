About Carmel High School

Founded in 1992, Carmel High School, Gadchiroli is guided by the CMI motto, "Guru Vachanam Mama Deepam" (Word of the Guru is my Light). We are committed to holistic education that blends academic rigor with strong character formation, developing disciplined, responsible, and socially conscious individuals.

Our nurturing environment, aligned with the CBSE curriculum, focuses on the all-round growth of each student. We aim to unlock every child's potential, fostering the intellectual, moral, and physical development needed to become well-rounded citizens prepared for life's creative and truthful challenges.

Academic Programs

Primary Section

Our Primary Section focuses on foundational learning with English Medium instruction, activity-based learning approaches, computer basics, and creative arts & crafts programs designed for young learners.

Secondary Section

The Secondary Section follows the State Board Syllabus offering both Science and Commerce streams, complemented by comprehensive sports training and diverse cultural activities for holistic development.

Co-curricular Activities

Our co-curricular program includes Scout & Guide training, various sports & games, music & dance classes, and access to fully equipped computer labs to enhance students' overall skills and interests.
